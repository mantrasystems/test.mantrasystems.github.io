About this course

  1. This training course is designed for QA/RA professionals who wish to produce EU MDR-compliant CERs in-house. It is also suitable for company founders and members of early stage start-ups.

  4. Confidently write CERs across your entire portfolio without needing to outsource work.

About our courses

  1. Our training courses are broken up into chapters and short, streamable videos which save your progress automatically. Where relevant, downloadable documents are included to support your learning.
