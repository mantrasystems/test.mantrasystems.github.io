About this course

  1. This training course is designed for people who want to produce complete Risk Management Systems in-house. It is suitable for QA/RA professionals with existing knowledge of the EU MDR and company founders during early stage product development.

  2. Instant, on-demand and unlimited access to videos and downloads along with updates and additions to the existing training material.

  3. This course is presented by the CEO of Mantra Systems, Dr Paul Hercock, who has extensive knowledge of medical device Risk Management. He is an experienced presenter of clinical and technical training.

  4. Confidently build a complete Risk Management System from start to finish for your entire portfolio of medical devices.

About our courses

  1. Our training courses are broken up into chapters and short, streamable videos which save your progress automatically. Where relevant, downloadable documents are included to support your learning.

  2. All our training courses are on-demand and can be completed to your schedule in the comfort of your own workspace. You won't have travel or accommodation costs, or any other expenses usually incurred whilst away from your office.

  3. You will have unlimited access to your selected training under a Single User Licence. If we update or add anything to the training course material, we will let you know by email. If you require access for multiple team members, please contact us about our Multi User Licences.

  4. Our EnableCE platform offers training courses and technical document templates that work together to provide a great regulatory support package.
