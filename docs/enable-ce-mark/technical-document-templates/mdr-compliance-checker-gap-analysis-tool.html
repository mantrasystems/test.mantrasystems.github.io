In more detail

Apply a simple and effective EU MDR Gap Analysis technique suitable for all medical device manufacturers.

  1. It is designed for any member of a medical device company with responsibility for ensuring alignment with EU MDR requirements. It does not require specialist regulatory knowledge.

  2. The tool has nine sections which are as follows;

    1. General requirements
    2. GSPR Conformity
    3. PMS (Post-Market Surveillance)
    4. PMCF (Post-Market Clinical Follow-up)
    5. Clinical Evaluation
    6. Risk Management
    7. Other regulatory processes
    8. Annex II Technical Documents
    9. Special Cases

  3. Quickly perform an analysis of your regulatory strategy and identify important gaps that need to be addressed for EU MDR conformity. It will produce a personalised scorecard precisely identifying areas in need of focus.

  4. Please see the Gap Analysis comparison chart.

  5. The tool is a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet and requires no activation or access codes. You will be able to reuse the template for all your medical devices.
